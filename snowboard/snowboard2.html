Fresh Powder

This snowboard is specially designed with a tail so that it will glide better on freshly fallen snow, this snowboard should only be used by those who are more experienced since this is for a specific type of skill set.

Freestyle board

This board is a freestyle board and was designed as an all rounder. If you are new to snowboarding or like to do a variety of boarding, this is the best snowboard.

Park skiis

If you are looking to get into the ski park, then these skiis are the best choice. They are specially designed to withstand hard impact and are easier to manuver, with that being said, they will not be optimal on fresh powder/long ski runs

Fresh powder skiis

these skiis are best in fresh powder. Unlike the park skiis, these are easier to find new paths and are prefect for longer runs.
